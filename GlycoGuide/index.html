*Global Impact*: Diabetes affects over 400 million people worldwide, with a significant increase in prevalence over the past few decades. *Health Implications*: Uncontrolled diabetes can lead to serious complications such as heart disease, kidney failure, and vision loss. *Economic Burden*: The economic cost of diabetes management and treatment is substantial, impacting healthcare systems and individuals. *Nutritional Complexity*: Diabetic patients face challenges in understanding the nutritional content of their meals and making informed choices. *Blood Sugar Management*: Maintaining stable blood sugar levels requires careful meal planning and monitoring. *Lifestyle Impact*: Diabetes can significantly impact a person's lifestyle, leading to dietary restrictions and the need for constant vigilance.
